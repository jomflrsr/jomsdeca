"IT DAYS 2025"

The IT Days 2025 are in full swing, bringing excitement and fierce competition to various events. In Computer Laboratory 4, participants are putting their typing skills to the ultimate test in the Blindfold Typing Contest, typing with speed and accuracy while relying on muscle memory. In Computer Laboratory 1, puzzle enthusiasts are engaged in a high-stakes BlockBlast Tournament, racing to stack, strategize, and clear blocks in rapid succession. Meanwhile, teams are battling it out in the Mobile Legends tournament inside K201 and K202, where strategic plays and split-second decisions determine the fate of each match.

Skilled programmers are competing in a thrilling Programming Contest in Computer Laboratory 2, tackling challenging coding problems under time pressure. In another corner of the laboratory, participants face off in the Battle of the Brain quiz bowl, answering IT-related questions in a high-energy battle of wits. Over at the Upper Gym, the Sumobot competition heats up as students showcase their self-built robots, each one crafted with precision for battle.

Earlier, the crowd at the Upper Gym cheered for contestants in the Cosplay Competition, where participants portrayed characters from popular anime series like *Spy x Family*, *Chainsaw Man*, and *Demon Slayer*. The competition set the stage for the grand culmination of IT Days, which will feature the highly anticipated Mr. and Ms. IT event. With contestants dressed to impress, this year's IT Days is a thrilling celebration of talent, technology, and creativity.

As IT Days 2025 continues to captivate with its exciting array of competitions, it’s clear that the event is a showcase of both talent and innovation. From the rapid keystrokes of the Blindfold Typing Contest to the strategic battles in Mobile Legends and the precision of self-built sumobots, the energy in each event is unmatched. With fierce competition in every corner, participants are proving their expertise and pushing their limits in various fields of technology. The culmination of IT Days promises to be just as thrilling, with the spotlight on Mr. and Ms. IT, where grace and glamour meet the world of tech. It’s a celebration of both intellect and creativity, marking another successful year for IT Days.
